Ecommerce Industry — Head of Technology & Systems (Miles)

Industry & Role

Industry: Ecommerce — marketplaces, payment processors, logistics, and platform providers.

Role: Head of Technology & Systems (Miles) — lead platform architecture, integrations, site reliability, and internal tooling.

Why this role?

Why: They have the strongest technical background and can coordinate platform, security, and automation efforts.

Responsibilities

  • Build and maintain the e‑commerce platform (website, integrations, APIs).
  • Manage IT infrastructure, cybersecurity, and data analytics.
  • Automate workflows (inventory syncing, order tracking, reporting).
  • Support logistics with tracking systems and warehouse software.
  • Provide technical support to the team.

Ethical Issue

Customer data monetization: balancing analytics/ads revenue against user privacy and consent (clear opt‑ins and anonymization required).

Cybersecurity Issues

Common risks that could arise in this organization:

  • Data breach: Exposure of customer PII or payment tokens from insecure storage or misconfigurations.
  • Supply-chain / third-party compromise: A vulnerable payment gateway, analytics script, or library introducing a backdoor.
  • Other risks: credential stuffing against customer accounts, phishing targeting staff, and DDoS affecting availability.

Best Practices

  • Enforce strong access control and MFA for all admin and vendor accounts.
  • Tokenize and never store raw card data; follow PCI‑DSS and use end‑to‑end encryption.
  • Harden and monitor APIs (rate limits, auth, input validation); keep dependencies and servers patched.
  • Regular vulnerability scans, pentests, and a practiced incident response plan.
  • Security training for staff to reduce phishing and social engineering risk.
