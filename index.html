What Are My Personal Values?

1. Integrity

Why is this important to you? Integrity is important to me because doing the right thing even when no one is looking is important to me, theres been times when I havent practiced integrity and its come back to me.

How did you develop this value? When I started to be a shift lead at my first job. If you dont do the right thing when no one is looking it will come back to haunt you and never in a good way.

2. Perserverance

Why is this important to you? Perserverance is important to me bacause when something is hard you shouldnt just give up. Power through it and take that reward thats yours.

How did you develop this value? I took a class in highschool, AP Computer Science Principles. This class was ver hard for me, I felt like I wasnt getting the material and knew for sure that I wasnt gonna get the AP credit. Halfway through the course something clicked and told myself I need to study and pass this exam. I started practicing a lot more and somehow got the AP credit.

3. Kindness

Why is this important to you? Kindness is important to me because why be mean in a world where everyone has problems you never know what saying "Hello (compliment))" to someone and how it will effect the rest of their day, week, etc.

How did you develop this value? I developed this value overtime. I have always been a nice person in my opinion but now going to highschool into college you just never know what issues or problems people have so be nice.
